How predictions work

Every arrival time shown is the official LTA estimate, corrected by a gradient-boosting model trained on how buses at this stop have actually behaved — by hour, day, crowding and bus type.

Ground truth comes from tracking each bus across successive polls until it arrives. The model retrains itself continuously.
